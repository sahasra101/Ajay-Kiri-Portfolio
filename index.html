About Me

Thank you for visiting my webpage. I am currently a student in the Trilogy/UPenn Full Stack BootCamp. I have been using computers since I was 10 years old and have been a big fan of technology my entire life. I have always wanted to learn how to code and develop software, and now to that end, I am making the jump into writing my own code rather than just being an end user.

I have a varied background and have worked in a variety of fields including: retail sales, chemist, biochemist, molecular biologist, scientist, personal trainer, teacher and lecturer, physician, acupuncturist. I am also fond of several hobbies including cooking, health and fitness, traveling and volunteering.
